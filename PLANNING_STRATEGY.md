## Principle

**As little design as possible — less, but better.**

Every tier must justify its existence. If it can be removed, remove it. If two can merge, merge them. The goal is radical simplicity grounded in the lived experience of the person this is built for.

---

## Process & order

1. **Person → Problem → Solution** (Tiers 0–2): establish what is true and what we're building.
2. **Rams Polish → Review** (Tiers 3–4): cut the concept ruthlessly, then check the cut held.
3. **Rams UX → Review Again** (Tiers 5–6): cut the interaction ruthlessly, then check again.
4. Only when **0–6 are blessed and convergent** does implementation earn the right to exist.

**All seven segments are planning. Zero code.**
