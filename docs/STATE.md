## Key insights this session (do not lose)

1. **Emotional blindness is inward AND outward.** Somatic-only perception (needles, warmth, flow; with temperature, motion, shape) but **no mapping to emotion names**. Rich somatic input, absent label = center of Problem 1. Emerged ~2022 (felt *nothing* before) → the capacity is **emergent, possibly still growing**.
2. **Unread intent defaults to threat.** Absence of readable intent fills with worst-case intent → paranoia; his directness is misread as rudeness. Aida's first job: replace a threat-default guess with a grounded read.
3. **Problem 2's true center: he saves facts, not emotions.** His words: *"I can save pictures, names, phone numbers — I cannot save their emotions."* Mechanism: sharp emotion → snapshot → reset; no video. Encoding is gated by emotional salience, which Problem 1 removes — so **Problem 1 is upstream of Problem 2.** Aida must be two things at once: an emotional **translator** (reads what they mean now) and an emotional **bank account** (a layered ledger per person, both directions — the impression they left on him + he left on them). *"Remember THEM, not their words."* Translator and memory-harness are the same act.
4. **Theory-of-mind is paramount** — the core engine, per-person, perspective-aware (what they know / what they think of themselves / where that diverges from how Dima sees them). **Breadth through depth:** serve the spectrum by learning each individual, never an "average neurodivergent" persona. (Position recorded in [../.claude/reasoning_ledger.md](../.claude/reasoning_ledger.md).)
5. **Irreducible core (Rams):** {per-person model, the incoming read, the outgoing wound-catch} + auto-memory. **Deferred:** Somatic Practicing (secondary mode), full perspective-divergence model, Chrome extension (Phase 2). **Promoted to required at Review:** cold-start humility + confidence-gating.
